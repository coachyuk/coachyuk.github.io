Nirvana

The island's top lodging and only four star resort Nirvana will help you to put all out all of the mental fires from home and truly relax to a level few have experienced. There is an internal restaurant offering local flavors and drink fair. The spa is available without an overnight stay and is a great way to work all the kinks out after a zipline or volcano adventure!

Hotels

There are many choices for hotels around Taniti. Take the time to plan out the important activities for your visit and pick a hotel that works well for your plans. A popular choice in hotels is the Blue Sky Lagoon located in the heart of Merriton Landing within walking distance of many attractions.

Bed & Breakfasts

Bed and breakfast options are plentiful and fit a variety of budgets. The current featured option is fitting to a mid level budget ran by a local family that has a wealth of knowledge of the area and what not to miss. Eggs and Becky is one of several great B&B options to choose from.

The Gathering

The lowest cost to stay in Taniti is at The Gathering the island's lone hostel. An easy place to get to just at the outside edge of Merriton Landing. They offer a low cost stay and also directly offer bicycle and helmet rental. Remember that the local helmet law is strictly enforced! Staying here simplifies many of the logistics.

Electricity

The power outlets on the island of Taniti are the same 120 volt outlets which are run in the United States. Adaptors can be acquired at a variety of retailers that are small enough to be portable and part of luggage.
