Beaches

Beach time is both an enjoyable time filler and one of the best ways to relax and recover from some of the other adventures of the island. The most popular beaches on the island are those around Yellow Leaf Bay. Yellow Leaf Bay has sandy beaches and is the central hub of the island.

Rainforest

The rainforest of the island is one of the most popular attractions. The rainforest is quite an experience all by itself between amazing sight seeing, and some exquisite opportunities for animal observation. Within the rainforest is a zipline adventure that makes for an even more exciting way to experience it.

Volcano

Taniti island is home to a small active volcano. Feed Pak Fi is a group of volcanologists who both study and run educational tours of the volcano. Taking a full day tour with volcanologists is a monumental experience of sight seeing, learning, and a moderate hike.

Snorkeling

Sea-Scapes is a snorkeling school offering courses in snorkeling with PADI instructors. The courses are designed for those with as basic a skillset as to be comfortable in water, all the way up to those who are ready to pursue scuba adventures. Amatuer snorkeling can be easily accomplished at Yellow Leaf Bay also.

Museum

The local history museum exhibits the history and culture of local fish, and fishermen over time. Fishery is still a major economical backbone of Taniti, even as tourism continues to grow. There is also a large rice farming exhibit that highlights the agricultural background central to the island's history.
