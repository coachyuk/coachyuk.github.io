Local Flavor

Of the 10 restaurants on the island, 5 serve a local cuisine. Each of these specialize in various local fish and rice dishes. The current featured restaurant is also home to the most popular dance floor on the island aptly named Akiu no Taue Odori which translates as "rice planting dance" is an experience.

Pan-Asian

There are two Asian restaurants. Thai Landing has a relaxing lounge atmosphere with many flavorful noodle and rice based dishes. The second has India-inspired flavors. Come enjoy some awakening flavors at Curry Den. Their Vindaloo may be the best taste experience on the island.

American Style

The island has three American Style restaurants. Burger Bar is a popular meeting spot with traditional burger fair, and with its own brewery. A little more upscale dining with an obvious star dish at Steaked Claim. A more relaxed, fast and easy stop at Willie's Diner will hit the spot.

Grocery Options

There are a number of local holidays that close the local attractions and restaurants. There are two large supermarkets with wide offerings of most of the things you forgot to bring. There are also two smaller grocers with more local offerings, and one 24 hour convenience store.

Alcohol

Legal drinking age on the island of Taniti is 18. This is not strictly enforced. The rule that is strictly enforced is that alcohol is neither served nor sold from Midnight to 9:00a.m.
